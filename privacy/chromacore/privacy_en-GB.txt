ChromaCore Privacy Policy
Last updated: 30 April 2026

Thanks for playing ChromaCore. This policy explains what data is processed, why, and what choices you have.

1. Controller
ChromaCore
Privacy contact: chamod3v@gmail.com

2. Scope
This policy applies to the Android game ChromaCore.

3. Data we may process
A) Game data (local)
- High score, available energy, last daily recharge date, tutorial progress and audio settings.

B) Google Play Games data (if signed in)
- Google Play Games player identifier and profile name.
- Score submission to leaderboard and related metadata.

C) Advertising and monetisation data (Google AdMob)
- Advertising ID / AD_ID and device identifiers used by Google ad SDKs.
- Ad request, load, impression and interaction signals for rewarded ads.
- Approximate location inferred by network/IP (no precise GPS from this app).

D) First-party analytics telemetry
- None. ChromaCore does not send first-party analytics telemetry. There is no Firebase Analytics or equivalent embedded in the app.

4. Purposes
We process data to:
- Provide core gameplay and local progress.
- Enable Google Play Games features (sign-in and leaderboard).
- Show rewarded ads and measure ad performance.
- Prevent abuse, fraud and policy violations.

5. Consent and privacy choices (EEA, UK, Switzerland)
Where legally required, we request consent via Google UMP (User Messaging Platform).
You can reopen privacy options from the in-game Privacy button on the main menu.

Important behaviour in this app:
- If consent is granted or not required, ads may operate normally.
- If consent is not granted, non-personalised/limited ads may still be shown, depending on Google settings and availability.

6. Third-party services
ChromaCore uses the following Google services:
- Google Play Games Services
- Google AdMob

Google's processing is governed by Google's own policies:
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google Play Games Services Terms: https://developer.android.com/games/pgs/terms
- AdMob Policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

7. Data sharing
We do not sell personal data.
Data required for Google Play Games features and ad monetisation may be shared with Google as service provider.

8. Storage and retention
- Local game data remains on your device until app data is deleted or app is uninstalled.
- Google Play Games and AdMob data may be retained according to Google policies.
- Email support requests may be retained as needed to handle your request and legal obligations.
